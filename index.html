How It Works

From hazard spotted to proof-of-fix — in under a minute.

Safely Track is designed for the field, not the conference room. Four steps. No training. Works for your whole crew via SMS.

1

Capture the hazard

When you spot something, open Safely Track on your phone and log it — with a photo if you have one, or without if the situation calls for moving fast. The whole thing takes less than 60 seconds.

No photo? No problem. You can record a "no photo" observation and flag it as a high-risk situation needing immediate action.
2

Standardize the record

Once you log the hazard, Safely Track auto-generates a consistent description and assigns a risk tier (Low / Medium / High). You review it, edit if needed, and confirm. Every observation looks the same — whether it's your most experienced super or a first-week employee logging it.

This is what makes your closeout record defensible. Consistent documentation across sites and crews.
3

Assign the fix — via SMS

You assign a corrective action to an owner. They get a text message with the hazard write-up, what needs to be fixed, and a link to submit proof when it's done.

No app download. No login. No training required. Works for General Contractor crews and subcontractors alike.

When the fix is complete, the owner submits a photo. That becomes your proof-of-fix — timestamped and attached to the original observation.
4

Track it all on your dashboard

Your Safety Director sees every open item across all sites in real time. The dashboard surfaces repeat hazard patterns so you can coach your teams before something goes wrong — not after.

When an audit comes, your closeout packet is ready to export. No scramble. No missing records.
